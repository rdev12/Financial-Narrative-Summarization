 eurodis electron annual report 2003 01
achievements
Eurodis Electron PLC has achieved the strategic goals 
it set in positioning itself for future growth:
• Centralised warehousing from eight separate country
warehouses to a new advanced logistics centre rated 
as the best in the industry, capable of handling significant
volume increases.
• Centralised Group purchasing operations giving increased
operational and cost efficiencies.
• Integrated all countries onto one common IT platform.
• Operational excellence through the creation of an integrated
infrastructure that consistently delivers a world class service 
at a low fixed cost.
• Rationalised our supplier focus to 27 key and 
80 complementary suppliers covering all technologies.
• Increased operational gearing, ready to take advantage 
of a market upturn and future growth.
