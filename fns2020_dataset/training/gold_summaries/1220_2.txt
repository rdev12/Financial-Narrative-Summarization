ARM Holdings plc, the world’s leading semiconductor
intellectual property (IP) supplier is at the heart of the
development of digital electronic products.
The continuing evolution of electronic devices has escalated to
the extent that the use of IP is now providing the technological
foundation for nearly every electronic device in the world today.
(Source: Gartner Dataquest’s 2004 IP company rankings)
