Plan, design, enable 
is what we do.
Plan
The challenges facing our
clients are multidimensional,
often because of the increasingly
complex modern environment.
From cost and risk planning,
feasibility studies and logistics,
to impact assessments and
stakeholder engagement
activity, we plan every aspect 
of our clients’ projects.
Design
Atkins designs intellectual
capital such as management
systems and business processes.
We also design physical
structures such as office towers,
schools, bridges and highways.
Whatever we design, we apply
the same passion and creativity
combined with rigorous 
quality standards.
Enable
Our clients choose Atkins
because they want to focus 
on their core operations. They
entrust us to look after the
management of projects,
people and issues – ensuring
that deadlines are met, costs
are controlled and success 
is delivered.
