mental health conditions

Supporting people with

present at birth such

Acquired brain injury
Helping those people with  
a brain injury from, for 
example, an accident or 
trauma to the brain, to recover 
and re-acquire skills lost  
as a result of their injury,  
and subsequently lead as 
independent a life as possible. 
We provide these services  
in hospital or community 
based environments.
Deaf
Providing specialist care  
for children who are deaf  
or have significant hearing 
impairment and associated 
challenging behaviours. 
Fostering
Providing care and emotional 
support to children and  
young people who cannot  
be cared for by their own 
parents or family.
We provide these services  
in specialist residential 
homes, schools, colleges  
and hospitals with specialist 
staff to support each  
setting. We also support 
these children through  
foster parents.
