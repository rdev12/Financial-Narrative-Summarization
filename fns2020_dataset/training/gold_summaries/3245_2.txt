CERES POWER HOLDINGS PLC ANNUAL REPORT 2008
Highlights
£20 million investment by Centrica plc at 300p per share  
for 10% equity stake 
£5 million funded CHP development and trialling 
programme secured with British Gas
British Gas volume forward order for a minimum  
of 37,500 CHP products
Strong balance sheet with £27.5 million in cash  
at 30 June 2008 
Brian Count, ex-Innogy plc CEO, appointed  
Non-Executive Chairman
UK Chairmen of Siemens and Motorola appointed  
as Non-Executive Directors February 2008
Key Achievements
Performance of core technology manufactured from 
scaleable processes independently confirmed 
1kW Fuel Cell Module achieved performance, size  
and weight targets 
CHP Alpha design phase for British Gas completed
Volume suppliers selected for key balance of plant 
components and equipment
