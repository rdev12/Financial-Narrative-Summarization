Annual report and accounts 2010 Corporate statement
Pure Wafer is an industry-leading, provider 
of silicon wafer reclaim services. Pure Wafer 
cleans and polishes silicon wafers sent 
for reclaim by many of the world’s largest 
semiconductor manufacturers. Pure Wafer 
operates state-of-the-art foundries in the 
UK and the US which have the capability 
to meet the stringent quality standards 
required by its customers.
Pure Wafer is listed on the Alternative 
Investment Market (AIM) of the London Stock 
Exchange. Headquartered in the UK, it operates 
on a global basis through an international sales 
force throughout Europe, North America and
