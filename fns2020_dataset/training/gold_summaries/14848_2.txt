 01 Star Energy Group plc Annual Report and Accounts 2006
Highlights 2006
Operational
The Humbly Grove gas store handed over to the customer, Vitol SA, 
for full customer nominations with effect from 1 February 2006
The Group adopted the Gas Act 1965 as the preferred approach 
for all of its future gas storage planning applications, with the 
expectation that it will provide enhanced certainty of planning 
application timelines
Proven and probable oil and gas reserves independently assessed  
at 25.1 million boe as at 31 December 2006 (2005: 26.3 million boe)
Average oil production for the year was 4,093 bopd  
(2005: 4,178 bopd weighted average oil production, including  
the Pentex acquisition) 
In March 2007, the Royal Society for the Prevention of Accidents 
(RoSPA) granted Star Energy the Gold Award for Occupational  
Safety for 2006
Financial
T urnover of £57.1 million (2005: £24.7 million)
Profit before tax of £14.6 million (2005: £2.1 million)
Basic earnings per share of 4.72 pence per ordinary share  
(2005: 1.82 pence)
