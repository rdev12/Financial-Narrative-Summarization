Management took timely and decisive  > >
steps early in Q4 2008 to deal with the 
unprecedented economic downturn:
• production cutbacks implemented 
(approximately 35% for ferroalloys  
and 50% for iron ore);
• labour and raw material costs reduced; 
• expansionary capex projects deferred  
(total capex in 2008 was US$1.3 billion); 
and
• first full year dividend (paid and proposed) 
of 31 US cents per share.
