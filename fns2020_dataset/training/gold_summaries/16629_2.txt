 Annual Report and Accounts 2007
1
Financial highlights
● Revenue increased by 15% to £12.7m (2006: £11.0m), representing record growth, on a
constant currency basis, turnover increased by 18% 
● EBITDA (earnings before interest, tax, depreciation and amortisation) increased by 40% to
£1.9m (2006: £1.3m)
● Operating profit rose 30% to £1.3m (2006: £1.0m) 
● Pre-tax profit of £1.1m (2006: £0.9m) 
● Basic earnings per share of 1.79p (2006: 1.51p), an increase of 19%
Operating highlights
● Successful integration of previous acquisitions resulting in the worldwide introduction of two new
product sectors covering plastic flow analysis and complex laser cutting
● A successful re-branding to the modernised Vero Software image which has brought significant
additional recognition and acclaim
