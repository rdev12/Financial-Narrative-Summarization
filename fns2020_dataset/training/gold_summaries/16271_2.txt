 The year ended 31 March 2003 has seen the
successful implementation of the first year of our
three year plan.
Financial
•
Turnover up by 18.7% to £156.3 million
•
Profit before tax up by 6.6% to £16.1 million
•
Earnings per share up by 6.0% to 21.2p
•
Dividends up 5.6% to 16.9p per share
Operational
•
Successful implementation of new business unit structure, with market 
share gains in all business units
•
Improved customer retention levels through focus on on-time 
delivery performance
•
Expansion and strengthening of the franchise network
•
Complementary new services added through the acquisition of 
technical courier BXT
