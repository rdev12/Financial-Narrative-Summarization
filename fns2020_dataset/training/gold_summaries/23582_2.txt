Our Business today
In simple terms, the Group provides 
Motor and Home insurance products  
and services through its two core  
brands – esure and Sheilas’ Wheels. 
The Group was founded in 2000 by 
Chairman, Peter Wood, and has since 
gone on to be one of the UK’s leading 
insurers with just under two million 
in-force policies.
