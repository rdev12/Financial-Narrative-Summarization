Financial Highlights
• Assets under influence (AUI) up 75% to £2.1bn  
(2015: £1.2bn)
• Revenue up 47% to £11.6m (2015: £7.9m) of which  
more than 75% is recurring
• Gross margin of 61% (2015: 56%)
• Adjusted EBITDA* is up 42% to £2.7m (2015: £1.9m)
• Net cash generated by operations of £2.4m  
(2015: £1.8m) and total cash balances at the period  
end of £10.5m (2015: £3.9m)
• Seventeen acquisitions completed during the period  
for an aggregate consideration of £11.7m (or 1.9% of AUI)
• Launch of own investment platform in partnership  
with AJ Bell
• IPO on AIM in March 2016 raising £10m of capital  
before expenses
*  Earnings before interest, taxation, depreciation and amortisation (EBITDA) is a non  
IFRS measure which the Group uses to assess its performance. 
*  Adjusted EBITDA is earnings before interest, taxation, depreciation, amortisation  
and exceptional costs.
