Dixons Retail plc 2 Annual Report and Accounts 2012/13
We are a leading European specialist electrical retailing and services company.  We trade through over 
1,100 stores and on-line. We operate four divisions as follows:
UK & Ireland Northern Europe Southern Europe PIXmania

are the largest specialist 
electrical retailing and 
services operators in the 
UK and Ireland.

brand.

countries.
