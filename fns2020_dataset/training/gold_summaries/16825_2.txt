We are a leading provider of interconnect solutions

datacoms/datacentre, industrial, consumer and 
medical sectors.
Volex’s products and solutions are integral to the 
increasingly sophisticated digital world in which 
we live, providing power and connectivity across 
a wide range of markets.
Whether at work, at play or on the move, 
Volex’s products are never far away.
