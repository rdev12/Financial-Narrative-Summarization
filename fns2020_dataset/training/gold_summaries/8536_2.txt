ITE Group plc Annual Report 2007
ITE would like to thank all those who participated in producing this report,
particularly the members of staff for their contributions.
This report is printed on Hello Silk paper , which is FSC certified (FSC Mixed
Sources product group from well managed forests and other controlled
sources) and is produced at a mill that is certified to the ISO14001 and
EMAS environmental management standards. The pulp is bleached
using mainly a T otally Chlorine Free (TCF) process, but some is bleached
using an Elemental Chlorine Free (ECF) process. This material is recyclable
and biodegradable.

please pass it on to other interested readers, return it to ITE or dispose
of it in your recycled paper waste.
