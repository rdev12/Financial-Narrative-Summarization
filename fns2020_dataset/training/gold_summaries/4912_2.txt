manufacturers of coding and marking and 
digital printing solutions and is publicly 
traded on the London Stock Exchange. 
Our products are used for date coding, 
product marking, serialisation and printing

Our customers are some of the most 
recognisable brands in healthcare, food, 
beverage and commercial printing.
