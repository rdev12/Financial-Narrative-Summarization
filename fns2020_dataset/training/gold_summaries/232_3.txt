Employees
ACM continues to recognise that the Group’s strength lies in 
itsbrokersandsupportstaff.Throughourexpansionintothedry
cargomarketsincetheperiodend,ACMisfurtherexpandingthe
teamandtheBoardcontinuestobededicatedtoensuringthat
thebrokingdesksarefullyintegratedandthatthebrokersacross
the Group are working together. The Board would like to thank all 
brokersandsupportstafffortheircontributionstoanotheryearof
significantachievement.
