(a)
Underlying profit is calculated as profit (or loss) excluding profit (or loss) on sale of fixed assets, external transaction costs and exceptional items.
(b)
Underlying earnings per share is calculated as profit (or loss) on ordinary activities after taxation, before profit (or loss) on sale of fixed assets and external transaction costs and exceptional
items (all net of tax), divided by the weighted average number of Ordinary Shares in issue in the period.
(c) 
Cash generated from operations excludes external transaction costs and (in 2014) pension contributions made from the proceeds of debt issues.
(d) 
Non-cash charges resulting from the refinancing in 2014 led to a reported statutory loss in the comparative period.
(e) 
Interim dividend represents the interim dividend that was declared and paid in the period out of earnings generated in the same period.
(f) 
The final dividend represents the final dividend that was declared and paid in the period relating to the earnings generated in the previous period.
